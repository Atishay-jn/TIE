Frequently Ask Question

Expert answers to FAQs

Weather Act of God is covered in fire policy or not?

Yes, Fire Policy covers everything today. All kinds of Acts of God are covered. No matter what.

Have the fire insurance rates been revised from 1st January 2020 by IRDA?

Yes, the General Insurance Corporation of India along with Insurance Information Bureau of India, have release the circular for revision in prices. Because most of the risks were written by insuring companies at below par prices.

Is home Insurance expensive?

That’s a funny thing, it’s as cheap as 500 Rs monthly for upto 1 cr of sum insured. The best part is , if taken for long period of 10 years, price reduces to 300 Rs per month.

Does the Insurance company matter?

A deep dive into insurance companies would tell you that what truly matters is the insurance policy's terms and conditions. As long as you trust the prime insurer whom you are purchasing the policy from, The Insurance Experts is just a means to an end - a facilitator that is here to make the process of insurance hassle-free for you.
